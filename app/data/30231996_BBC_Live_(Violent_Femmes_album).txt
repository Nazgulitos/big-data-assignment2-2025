BBC Live is a live album by Violent Femmes recorded in May 1991 at The Town & Country Club in London for BBC Radio 1's In Concert series, and broadcast on 20 July 1991.BBC Genome Project - In Concert listing for 20 July 1991 It was released on Hux Records in 2005. ==Track listings== ==Personnel== * Gordon Gano – vocals, guitar * Brian Ritchie – bass, backing vocals * Victor DeLorenzo – drums, backing vocals ==References== Category:Violent Femmes live albums Category:2005 live albums Category:BBC Radio recordings
