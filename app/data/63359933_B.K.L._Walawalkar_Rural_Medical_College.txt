B.K.L. Walawalkar Rural Medical College is a full-fledged medical college in Sawarda, Maharashtra. The college imparts the degree Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS). It is recognised by the Medical Council of India. Selection to the college is done on the basis of merit through the National Eligibility and Entrance Test.
