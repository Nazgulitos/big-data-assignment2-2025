BDJ Team is a peer-reviewed online magazine published by Nature Research on behalf of the British Dental Association - of which it is a fellow publication of the latter's official journal, the British Dental Journal. The editor-in- chief is Stephen Hancocks. The journal replaced Vital, which was published between 2003 and 2013.
