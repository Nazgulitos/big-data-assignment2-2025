B D Behring (born 1 March 1946) is an Indian politician from Manipur belonging to the Janata Dal party. He was elected to the Rajya Sabha for the term, 10 April 1990 to 9 April 1996 from Manipur. However he resigned immediately. He has been elected to the Manipur Legislative Assembly from Chandel constituency, during 1990-1995 and 2000-2005.
