B. V. Abdulla Koya (1914–1998) was an Indian politician from Kerala. He was elected to Indian Parliament (Rajya Sabha) from Kerala several times (1967–1973, 1974–1980, 1980–1986, 1986–1992 and 1992–1998) with the Indian Union Muslim League. ==References== Category:1914 births Category:1998 deaths Category:Rajya Sabha members from Kerala Category:Indian Union Muslim League politicians
