B-Y indicates a color difference signal between Blue (B) and a Luminance component, as part of a Luminance (Y) and Chrominance (C) color model. It has different meanings depending on the exact model used: *U in YUV, a generic model used for analog and digital image formats; *Cb in YCbCr, used for digital images and video; *Pb in YPbPr, used in analog component video; *Db in YDbDr, used in analog SECAM and PAL-N; ==See also== R-Y
