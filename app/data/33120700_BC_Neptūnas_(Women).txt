Klaipėdos "Neptūnas" (Women) is the major women basketball club of Klaipėda, Lithuania participating in Lithuanian Women Basketball League and EuroCup Women. Klaipėdos "Fortūna" basketball club was founded in 2001 as Lemminkainen, in 2012 after end of sponsorship it was changed to "Fortūna".
