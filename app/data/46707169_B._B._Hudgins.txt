Brice Benjamin Hudgins (August 15, 1854 - December 1, 1923) was an American politician. He was a member of the Arkansas House of Representatives, serving from 1887 to 1893, and was Speaker of the House in his last term. He was a member of the Democratic party. He served as a circuit court judge from 1890 to 1898 and from 1908 to 1912. He was also chairman of the Arkansas Railroad Commission from 1903 to 1907. B. B.
