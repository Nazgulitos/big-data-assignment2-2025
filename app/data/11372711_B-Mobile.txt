B-Mobile Communications Snd. Bhd. (stylized b•mobile) was a joint venture company between Telekom Brunei Berhad (TelBru) and QAF Comserve. Granted a 3G license in February 2005, b.mobile was Brunei’s first 3G mobile service provider, utilizing the established UMTS technology.
