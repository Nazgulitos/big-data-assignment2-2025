BC Titebi Tbilisi () is the Georgian professional basketball club, that is based in Tbilisi, Georgia. The club competes in the Georgian basketball Super Liga. == History == The club was established by the Georgian basketball players and Georgia national basketball team members Giorgi Gamqrelidze and Besik Lezhava in 2016. In 2018, the club was promoted to the Superliga after beating Sokhumi in the promotion/relegation playoffs.
