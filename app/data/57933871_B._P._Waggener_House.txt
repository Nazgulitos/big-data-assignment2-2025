The B. P. Waggener House is a historic three-story house in Atchison, Kansas. It was built in 1884 for Balie P. Waggener, the general counsel of the Missouri Pacific Railroad. With It was sold out of the Waggener family in the 1950s. The house was designed in the Victorian architectural style.
