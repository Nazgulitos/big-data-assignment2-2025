B.V. Acharya ( B. Vasudeva Acharya) is senior advocate at Karnataka High court. He is son of Ramachandra Acharya, a Shivalli Madhva Brahmin and native of Udupi district. He has been advocate general of Karnataka government several times. He shot to prominence nationally in Disproportionate assets case against Jayalalithaa, where he pursued case as special public prosecutor and special counsel taking to its logical end in spite of hardship and threats.
