B Sides and C Sides is a compilation album by the American punk rock band Rancid. It was first released online on December 11, 2007,Punknews.org | Rancid to release "B Sides and C Sides" followed by a standard release on January 15, 2008. It contains a number of B-sides and rare songs as well as compilation or soundtrack appearances plus 4 previously unreleased songs. The set spans from 1992 to 2004, therefore it doesn't include any songs recorded with current drummer Branden Steineckert.
