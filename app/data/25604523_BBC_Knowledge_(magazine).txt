BBC Knowledge Magazine was a magazine covering science, nature and history which was launched in 2008; it closed in November 2012. The magazine's now- defunct website described it thus: > BBC Knowledge Magazine - the new magazine about science, nature and > history...invention, innovation and more. Sir Francis Bacon was right about > knowledge. It is power. Ben Franklin agreed, "An investment in knowledge > always pays the best interest." BBC Knowledge Magazine is unrelated to the TV channels BBC Knowledge and BBC Knowledge (international) The magazine was chosen as one of the top ten magazines launched in 2008 by Library Journal."Ten new launches buck the downturn", Library Journal, 1 May 2009 (Retrieved 29 December 2009).
