BD+03 2562 is a very-low-metallicity star in the constellation of Virgo. It is located about 8,500 light-years (2,600 parsecs) from Earth. ==Planetary system== The star is orbited by a superjovian exoplanet, BD+03 2562 b, which was discovered in 2017 by a radial velocity method. ==References== Category:K-type giants Category:Virgo (constellation) BD+03 2562 J11501555+0245365 Category:Planetary systems with one confirmed planet
